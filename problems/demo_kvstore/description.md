# In-Memory Key-Value Store

Build a simple in-memory key-value store, one level at a time. Each level
adds a capability on top of the previous one — and you won't see the next
level's spec until the current level's tests all pass.

This is the **demo problem** shipped with MockSignal so you can confirm the
app works end to end. Drop in a real practice problem (e.g. Bank System)
when you're ready.

Implement your work in `solution.py` (the center editor). Hit **Run Tests**
on the right to check your progress.
